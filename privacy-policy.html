Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Apex ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you use the Apex iOS app, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Apex is an experience-first driving app. We collect only what's needed to record your drives and improve your experience. We do not sell your data and we do not show ads.

1. Information We Collect

📍 Location Data

We collect GPS location data while you are actively recording a drive, including in the background when the app is minimised. Location data is used to map your route, calculate distance and speed, detect road types, and compute your Drive Score. We do not collect location data when you are not recording.

🚗 Vehicle Data

If you connect a vehicle through Smartcar, Tesla Fleet API, or an OBD-II Bluetooth adapter, we collect data such as speed, fuel level, battery percentage, engine RPM, odometer, and vehicle make/model. This data is used to enrich your drive records and power the in-app dashboard. You can disconnect your vehicle at any time in Settings.

👤 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, display name, and optional profile photo and bio. If you sign in with Apple, we receive only the information Apple provides (email and name). We use this information to create and maintain your profile.

🛣 Drive Records

We store the routes, times, distances, road types, and Drive Scores for drives you save. Drives you mark as private are visible only to you. Drives you mark as public may appear in the community feed.

📊 Usage Data

We collect basic crash reports and anonymised usage analytics (e.g. which screens are visited) to help us improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To record, display, and analyse your drives
  • To calculate your Drive Score and provide AI coaching feedback
  • To show your routes on the map and in your history
  • To power the community route discovery feed (public routes only)
  • To maintain your account and profile
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Privacy Zones

You can set Privacy Zones in the app (e.g. around your home). GPS tracking automatically pauses when you are within a Privacy Zone, and those portions of your route are hidden from your saved drive records and any shared content.

4. Third-Party Services

Apex uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — account authentication and data storage. Firebase Privacy
  • Smartcar — vehicle connectivity for supported manufacturers. Smartcar Privacy
  • Tesla Fleet API — vehicle connectivity for Tesla vehicles. Tesla Privacy
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — road classification data. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered coaching feedback on your drives. Drive summaries (no personally identifiable information) are sent for analysis. Anthropic Privacy
  • Apple MapKit — map display and route calculation. Apple Privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and saved drives are stored on Firebase servers. Vehicle OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. We use HTTPS for all network communications. We follow industry-standard practices to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and drive records for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated data (e.g. road usage statistics with no user identifiers) may be retained for longer.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your drive data in a machine-readable format (GPX)
  • Withdrawal of consent — disconnect your vehicle or delete your account at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable crash reporting in app Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Apex is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of your personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. Apex does not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us at the email address below.

10. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (for location and vehicle data) and the performance of our contract with you (for account and drive data). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
